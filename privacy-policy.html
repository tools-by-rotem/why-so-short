Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority

At Why so short? your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Chrome extension.

1. What data do we collect?

Why so short? does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or browsing data. All processing to resolve shortened URLs happens locally in your browser.

2. How do we use your data?

Since we do not collect any data, there is no usage or sharing of your information.

3. Third-party services

Why so short? does not use any third-party analytics or tracking services, and does not send any data to external servers.

4. Security

All link resolution is done inside your browser using built-in web APIs, ensuring your browsing data stays private and secure.

5. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. When we do, the updated version will be posted on our homepage with the new effective date.

6. Contact us

If you have any questions or concerns about your privacy, please contact us at rotemkanon.dev@gmail.com.
